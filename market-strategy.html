Go-To-Market
Strategy & Rollout

CiviWatch Guardian AI operates on a lean, scalable hybrid model. We do not burn capital building redundant AI engines; instead, we orchestrate existing, world-class 3rd-party AI and provide the essential layer of Human-in-the-Loop validation to establish the definitive safety benchmark.

The Two-Phase Rollout

Phase 1: The 'Lighthouse' Strategy

NGOs & Civil Society

We establish market authority by providing our platform to NGOs and advocacy groups (often free or subsidized). By empowering them to identify and flag systemic hate speech, we generate undeniable, data-backed reports on platform toxicity.

  • ▹ Establishes CiviWatch as an objective authority.
  • ▹ Creates regulatory pressure on social networks.
  • ▹ Perfects the Human-in-the-loop (HITL) workflows.
Phase 2: B2B Integration

Social Networks & Platforms

Facing mounting regulatory fines (e.g., EU DSA, UK Ofcom) and public pressure generated in Phase 1, platforms are offered CiviWatch as the ultimate B2B solution. We act as the outsourced benchmark, saving them massive internal development costs.

  • ▹ Direct API integration for automated orchestration.
  • ▹ Saves networks internal AI and moderation OPEX.
  • ▹ Grants platforms the 'ISO Digital Safety' mark.

Future Expansion Pivots

The ISO Standard model is highly adaptable. Beyond traditional social media, CiviWatch is positioned to regulate the next frontiers of digital interaction.

🤖

Generative AI Auditing

Providing safety benchmarks and bias testing for LLMs and image generation models to ensure EU AI Act compliance.

👓

Virtual Reality (VR) Safety

Creating behavioral benchmarks and spatial harassment detection protocols for the Metaverse and spatial computing platforms.
