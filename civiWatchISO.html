The Core Goal:
Digital Safety Standard

A critical part of our solution is introducing an Official Safety Standard (ISO-Like). This mark will serve as a universal quality stamp for platforms, indicating active monitoring and a protected environment. This initiative was established with the clear goal of raising the capital and resources needed to implement this standard globally.

⚠️ The Problem

  • Massive operational costs and slow response times against the volume of content.
  • Flood of hate speech, cyberbullying, and antisemitism damaging brands directly.
  • Mass user churn and advertiser withdrawal due to a loss of digital safety and trust.

🛡️ The Solution

  • Real-time threat detection using combined AI engines (Text & Visual) to neutralize threats.
  • Applying a digital safety standard ensuring a protected space for users.
  • Drastic reduction in OPEX, legal risk mitigation, and high user retention rates.
