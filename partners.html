The Core Goal: A Digital Safety Standard

A critical part of our solution is introducing an Official Safety Standard. This mark will serve as a quality stamp for platforms, indicating active monitoring and a protected environment. This initiative was established with the clear goal of raising the capital and resources needed to implement this standard globally.

⚠️ The Problem

  • Massive operational costs and slow response times.
  • Flood of hate speech and antisemitism damaging brands.

🛡️ The Solution

  • Real-time threat detection using combined AI engines (Text & Visual).
  • Applying a digital safety standard ensuring a protected space.
